Famous Places & Important Timings

Char Dham
Uttarakhand, India
  • Opening Hours:5:00 AM - 8:00 PM
  • Morning Aarti:6:00 AM - 7:00 AM
  • Evening Aarti:6:30 PM - 7:30 PM

The Char Dham refers to four sacred pilgrimage sites in Uttarakhand: Yamunotri, Gangotri, Kedarnath, and Badrinath. Each offers spiritual experiences and scenic beauty.

Haridwar
Uttarakhand, India
  • Ganga Aarti:6:30 PM - 7:30 PM
  • Temple Opening Hours:5:00 AM - 9:00 PM

Haridwar is a holy city where the Ganges River exits the Himalayas. The evening Ganga Aarti on the ghats is a major attraction.

Rishikesh
Uttarakhand, India
  • Morning Aarti at Triveni Ghat:6:00 AM - 7:00 AM
  • Evening Aarti:6:00 PM - 7:00 PM
  • River Rafting Slot 1:9:00 AM - 11:00 AM
  • River Rafting Slot 2:3:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Known as the Yoga Capital of the World, Rishikesh offers spiritual experiences, adventurous river rafting, and scenic beauty along the Ganges.

Mussoorie
Uttarakhand, India
  • Monsoon Special Offer:N/A
  • Best Visit Time:March - June

Popular hill station known for pleasant weather and scenic views. Ideal for trekking, sightseeing, and nature walks.
